# 📊 A/B Testing — Kindle Ad Campaigns

> Statistical comparison of two Kindle ad campaigns to optimize spend and maximize conversions.

---

## Campaigns Compared

| | AKIN9326 | AKIN8012 |
|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Higher clicks, spend & conversions | Higher CTR, lower cost per conversion |
| **Best for** | Engagement volume | Budget efficiency |
| **Top age group** | 35–49 | 30–34 |

**Both campaigns:** Females drove more conversions · Business & Industry was the top-converting interest group

---

## Verdict

- **AKIN9326** — best overall engagement and conversion volume, but at higher cost
- **AKIN8012** — more cost-efficient; ideal for tighter budgets
- **Next step:** Reduce conversion costs on AKIN9326 while preserving its engagement advantage

## Methodology

| Step | Detail |

| Data Cleaning | Duplicates, missing values, inconsistencies |
| EDA | Trend visualization with Pandas, Seaborn, Plotly |
